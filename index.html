UI Design - A Fitness App

I designed a new fitness app inspired by what I like most about other fitness apps. As I mainly use Samsung Health as my fitness tracker, it is common for me to be fed with a lot of information on a single screen. I like that, but I wanted to simplify it a bit with this app and put more effort into the appearance rather than the functionality because you still see enough fitness data at once with this app.

UI Animation - Spotify Launch Design

I redesigned the start animation of Spotify. This way the animations of launching Spotify is smoother. Also, I hided some elements in Spotify to make the home tab look cleaner and only show the necessary information - just expand any menu by clicking on the arrow icon.

3D Product Design - Google Pixel 8 Concept

I created a concept for the Pixel 8 in Blender because I am a big fan of Pixel smartphones and wanted to express my thoughts on how I think Google's smartphones should evolve in the next generation.

3D Product Design - Acrylic Keyboard

This is a keyboard I modelled in Blender. I really like acrylic frames and because my favourite color is violet, I used it for the RGB lights in the frame. I used the Cycles render engine like I do for most of my renders.
